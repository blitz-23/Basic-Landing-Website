Casio: Edifice

Description

Edifice are a brand of watches manufactured by the Japanese electronics company Casio. The Edifice watches are designed to attract buyers engaged in professional careers. The watches have multiple functions, ranging from multiple dials to world time and multiple alarms. These watches range from the basic Chronograph to more advanced models with more computerised movements. Many Edifice watches use Tough Solar. More advanced models are called 'Waveceptors' , i.e. they can calibrate themsellves with atomic clocks via radio waves. Some models have bluetooth connectivity which enable them to be monitored via a smartphone and to share their chronograph data. The display of the watches range from pure analogue display to the digital displays, to the hybrid ones.
